The Saga of Sly doesn't just revolve around Sly as a character. It centers around the universe he inhabits. As Sly's universe takes place on earth, many of the characters are of different species from different parts of the galaxy.

Influences

The Saga of Sly was conceived with a enormous amount of influences from Anime and pop culture.

Most notable influences include:

  • Dragonball Z
  • Yu Yu Hakusho
  • FlCl
  • Music and Swag From -
    • Lil B The Basedgod

Connecting the world of Anime and the curious mind of Lil B, the characters were intended to embody eccentricity, freedom, and the comedic relief provided by not taking yourself too serious.
